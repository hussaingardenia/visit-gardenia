Gardenia Season 4

A curated cultural and design experience at the Kuwait National Museum — celebrating nature, creativity, sustainability, and community.

About Gardenia

Gardenia Season 4 is designed as a calm and inspiring seasonal journey. Visitors explore thoughtfully crafted spaces featuring art installations, workshops, cultural activations, eco-conscious brands, and community-driven moments.

Every element of Gardenia is curated to reflect minimal design, harmony with nature, and meaningful human connection.

About Kayan Office

Gardenia is organized by Kayan Office, a creative and cultural team in Kuwait focused on building experiences that connect design, culture, sustainability, and community.

Founder: Bashar Al Salem — leading initiatives that elevate public experiences through thoughtful programming and intentional design.
