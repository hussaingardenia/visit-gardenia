Gardenia

Gardenia is a seasonal cultural destination hosted at the Kuwait National Museum, bringing together creativity, sustainability, and community through thoughtfully curated experiences.

Kayan Office

KAYAN is an architecture and construction company established by architect Bashar Al-Salem, offering a one-stop concept from design inception through build-out and delivery.

KAYAN projects are distinguished by innovative contemporary and vernacular concepts where simplicity and functionality meet local tradition and culture.

Since its foundation in 2006, KAYAN has completed over 100 projects ranging from private residences to large-scale commercial and cultural developments.
