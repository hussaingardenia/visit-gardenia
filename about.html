About Gardenia

Gardenia Season 4 is a curated cultural and design experience that brings together nature, creativity, sustainability, and community in one immersive destination.

Hosted at the Kuwait National Museum, Gardenia features art installations, workshops, cultural activations, eco-conscious brands, and thoughtfully designed spaces.

About Kayan Office

Gardenia is organized by Kayan Office, a Kuwait-based creative organization founded by Bashar Al Salem, focused on cultural programming, experiential design, and community-driven initiatives.
